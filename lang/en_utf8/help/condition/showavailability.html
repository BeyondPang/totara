Before Activity is Available

Before activity is available allows you to set how the restricted activity will display on the course page for learners.

  • Show activity greyed-out, with restriction information: The learner can see the activity, but is not able to launch the activity until the restriction conditions have been met. The restriction conditions are visible to the learner.
  • Hide activity entirely: The learner will not be able to see the activity until the restriction conditions have been met.
